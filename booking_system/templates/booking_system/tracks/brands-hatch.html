About Brands Hatch

Brands Hatch is one of the most iconic motor racing venues in the UK, nestled in the Kent countryside. With a rich history dating back to 1926, this legendary track has hosted Formula 1, British Touring Cars, Superbikes, and many more elite championships.

The circuit offers two configurations — the shorter "Indy Circuit" and the longer "Grand Prix Circuit" — known for their fast straights, blind corners, and dramatic elevation changes.

Brands Hatch is especially famous for its spectacular views for spectators and challenging driving dynamics, making it a top choice for track day enthusiasts.
